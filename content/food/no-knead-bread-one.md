## My first shot at baking bread
Proofing the yeast in a warm water with sugar. The foam means it's still alive.

![Brand of flour and year proofing.](../img/2018-01-26__no-knead-bread__proofing-yeast.jpg)

Mix flour and water well, then incorporate salt and yeast.

![Mixed dough.](../img/2018-01-26__no-knead-bread__mixed-dough.jpg)

Yeast doing its job.

![Risen dough.](../img/2018-01-26__no-knead-bread__risen-dough.jpg)

Proofing dough in a frying pan. Final rise is to allow the gluten to relax enough for a big rise to happen in oven.

![Proofing dough.](../img/2018-01-26__no-knead-bread__proofing-pan.jpg)

Flipped dough into a dutch oven. Cover and bake at ~450F.

![Before bake.](../img/2018-01-26__no-knead-bread__before-bake.jpg)

![Fresh Bread.](../img/2018-01-26__no-knead-bread__fresh-bread.jpg)

Success!
